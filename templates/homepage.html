About Us

PaperSetu is dedicated to simplifying the management of academic conferences. Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for conference chairs, authors, and reviewers, ensuring fair and efficient peer review and conference organization.

For Conference

Chairs can create and manage conferences, invite reviewers, and track submissions. Authors can join conferences and submit papers. Reviewers can volunteer and be invited to review papers, ensuring a robust peer review process.
